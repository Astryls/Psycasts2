Pilgrimages

The guided journey from Awakened to Illuminated runs through two quest chains. Each tier (II and III) is earned at a generated pilgrimage site, and you choose the route: a combat trial, or a pacifist journey.

Trial of the Altar - combat, single site

A single pilgrimage site with an ancient psycaster altar. The pilgrim meditates there while waves of Ancient Psycasters assail the site - a defended vigil. Survive and complete the meditation to claim the tier. Best for colonies that can field a fighting escort.

Way of the Anima - pacifist, multi-site

A wandering journey across several quiet sites, each with an anima tree or natural focus to meditate at - no waves, no threats. Slower and more exposed on the world map, but it never demands a fight, making it the route for pacifist runs and fragile psycasters.

How the chains behave

BehaviourDetail
Auto-acceptedWhen a pilgrimage becomes available it is accepted automatically and announced with a "Pilgrimage begins" letter - no offer to miss.
Coma-exemptPilgrimage maps suppress all psychic-coma risk, so the long daily meditation the quest requires can never backfire.
Threat-suppressedOn the pacifist route, infestations, ambushes and the like are blocked; the altar's scripted waves still spawn (they come from the quest, not the incident system).
Daily capPilgrimage meditation is capped per day, so the journey takes a sensible number of days rather than a single marathon.
Foci, fixedAnima trees are force-meditatable, foci never spawn in water or on infertile ground, and pilgrim foci use a requirement-free focus type any pawn can use.
Reaching Illuminated (Tier III) ends the guided pilgrimages - but not the journey. From there, continued meditation opens the endless Transcendent climb.
