Meditation & Awakening

Meditation is the engine of the mod. A dedicated colonist meditates to the Awakening; a psycaster meditates for breakthroughs and, beyond Illuminated, for Transcendence. But meditating around the clock courts the psychic coma - the system rewards rhythm, not grinding.

Clock primer: 2500 ticks = 1 in-game hour, 60000 ticks = 1 day. Progress only accrues while a pawn is actually meditating, not while walking to the spot.

The Awakening (non-psycasters)

A non-psycaster who meditates builds cumulative hours. Each hour rolls a chance to Awaken that ramps with total dedication and is guaranteed once they pass the guarantee threshold - so a committed colonist always reaches Tier I within about a week.

Hourly awakening chance
P = min( 0.08 + 0.02 · cumHours, 0.60 ) # forced once cumHours ≥ awakenGuaranteeHours (default 36)
cumHourslifetime meditation hours (never resets)
awakenGuaranteeHourshard guarantee - setting, default 36 (~a week of 6-hour days)

At the Awakening the colonist becomes a psycaster and opens the card pick to choose their first path.

Breakthroughs: a flow of ancient knowledge

An existing psycaster's meditation can trigger “a flow of ancient knowledge” - a full level in one burst. Its chance is streak-based and scaled down by saturation, so habitual all-day meditators get rarer breakthroughs than someone who meditates in measured sessions. Past Illuminated, breakthroughs speed up - but never become certain. Each Transcendent tier raises the multiplier (Transcendent I ×1.15 … Transcendent VII ×2.05), so deep Transcendents reach the ceiling with about half the streak (~15h vs ~39h) as leveling slows. The 0.60 hard cap always holds.

How the chance grows per tier

Tiers 0 through III (Illuminated) all roll at the same base chance - the per-tier boost is a Transcendence reward. From Transcendent I (tier IV) the tierMult climbs +15% each tier, so the same streak converts to a breakthrough far more often. It still cannot pass the 60% ceiling; deeper tiers simply reach it sooner.

Over-meditation & the psychic coma

Up to the safe window each day there is no risk. Past it, every additional hour adds coma risk, rolled hourly. A long unbroken streak adds a little more, and each deferred card re-roll adds a flat amount.

Coma risk per hour
risk = max(0, (todayH−safe)·r) + max(0, (streakH−safe)·r·0.5) + rerolls·0.06 # clamped to a maximum of 0.75 per roll
todayH / streakHhours meditated today / consecutively
safethe safe window - setting, default 6 hours/day
rcoma risk per excess hour - setting, default 5%

When a coma strikes, its length scales with level, how far past the window the pawn pushed, and accumulated saturation - so repeated abuse yields ever-longer comas.

Coma length (ticks)
len = 10000 + level·2500 + excessH·2500 + saturation·30000 # clamped between 4 hours and 8 days
Pilgrimage meditation is coma-exempt. The quests demand long days, and a coma there would sabotage the journey - so keep regular daily meditation at or under the safe window, but meditate freely on a pilgrimage map.

Foci & focus types

The meditation focus a pawn uses - a brazier's flame, a throne, an anima tree, a personal default - selects an exclusive bucket that biases their Awakening and tier card picks toward thematically-matching paths. Tier III draws and Transcendent picks ignore the bias and draw from every path. Thrones are usable without a royal title, and anima trees and pilgrim foci are always meditatable.
