Ascension Constellations

Beyond the specialization tree sit seven mutually-exclusive ascensions - grand lore identities, each with a signature psychic effect and a rendered aura that haloes the pawn. A psycaster may walk only one.

The seven

AscensionThemeSignature
Tranquil MindStillness, clarityA calm aura; serenity and unshakeable focus.
Archotech AscendantMachine-godhoodSteady psyfocus regeneration - the well never runs dry.
Umbral SovereignShadow, dominionAn umbral aura that suppresses and unnerves those nearby.
EmpyreanRadiant lightA blessing aura that buoys all allies in range, psycaster or not.
PandemoniumWrath, fireA roiling aura of destructive intensity.
ChronosTimeAccelerated psyfocus regeneration; the flow of time bends inward.
ConsonanceHarmony, unityAn aura that strengthens nearby psycasters - a choir of minds.
Each is reached through the matching specialization ascension path drifting above Synthesis. Three paths are named for their star-sign rather than their identity: Halcyon → Tranquil Mind, Ascendant → Archotech Ascendant, Penumbra → Umbral Sovereign.

Auras

Each ascension renders a continuous aura under the pawn, drawn with layered fleck effects (mandalas, glyphs, wisps, glows) tuned to its theme. The ally and umbral auras carry real radius effects, while every ascension drives the visual identity. Aura rendering can be toggled off per pawn for a quieter battlefield.

"Ascension" vs "Ascended" vs "Ascendance": the constellation identity here, the enlightenment Tier III "Ascended", and the level-cap Synthesis specialization are three different systems with three similar names.
