Path Cards & Foci

Every time a psycaster reaches a new enlightenment tier, they unlock a path from a spread of face-down cards. What appears in that spread is steered by the meditation focus they cultivated at. This page covers the three tier picks and how each focus type biases the draw.

Where the picks come from: Tier I is the Awakening; Tiers II and III are pilgrimage rewards; Tier IV and beyond are Transcendence. Embracing a card unlocks that whole path for free.

The three tier picks

The first three picks are the heart of the system. Each deals a different spread, and the focus bias only applies to the first two - by Tier III you are drawing from the entire library, rare schools included.

Why the bias fades. Early picks lean into your colony's theme so a fire-tender awakens to fire schools; later picks open the whole archive so an Ascended psycaster can reach anything. Wild cards in the early spreads keep a sliver of surprise even when steered.

Anatomy of a pick

The spread opens as face-down tarot cards in the Modern Psycasts tile style. You turn one card of your choice; it flips with a reveal burst, and its path is the one you commit to.

How each focus steers the draw

Each meditation focus type owns an exclusive bucket of schools - a school belongs to one focus only, so meditating at a given focus rolls just that thematic group (with one wild card for variety). With no focus type, the draw instead leans on the meditator's personality traits. This map is the curated theming; any school an addon adds that is not listed lands in an even fallback bucket.

Deal a spread

Pick a focus and a tier, deal, then turn a card. Tiers I and II steer toward the focus (blue-edged cards); Tier III ignores the focus and draws from the whole library. Deal again for a fresh spread.
