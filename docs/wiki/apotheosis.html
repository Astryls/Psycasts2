Apotheosis Constellations

Beyond the specialization tree sit seven mutually-exclusive Apotheosis - grand lore identities, each with a signature psychic effect and a rendered aura that haloes the pawn. A psycaster may walk only one.

The seven

ApotheosisThemeSignature
Tranquil MindStillness, clarityA calm aura; serenity and unshakeable focus.
Archotech SingularityMachine-godhoodSteady psyfocus regeneration - the well never runs dry.
Umbral SovereignShadow, dominionAn umbral aura that suppresses and unnerves those nearby.
EmpyreanRadiant lightA blessing aura that buoys all allies in range, psycaster or not.
PandemoniumWrath, fireA roiling aura of destructive intensity.
ChronosTimeAccelerated psyfocus regeneration; the flow of time bends inward.
ConsonanceHarmony, unityAn aura that strengthens nearby psycasters - a choir of minds.
Each is reached through the matching specialization Apotheosis path drifting above Convergence. Three paths are named for their star-sign rather than their identity: Halcyon → Tranquil Mind, Singularity → Archotech Singularity, Penumbra → Umbral Sovereign.

Auras

Each Apotheosis renders a continuous aura under the pawn, drawn with layered fleck effects (mandalas, glyphs, wisps, glows) tuned to its theme. The ally and umbral auras carry real radius effects, while every Apotheosis drives the visual identity. Aura rendering can be toggled off per pawn for a quieter battlefield.

Three distinct systems: the seven Apotheosis constellations here, the enlightenment Tier III "Illuminated", and the level-cap Convergence specialization are separate endgame progressions, now named distinctly so they never blur together.
