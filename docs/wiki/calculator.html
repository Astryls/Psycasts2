Build Planner

Plan a psycaster end to end: set a level and tier, walk the constellation, pick a school and level its psycasts, and watch the specialization points, skill points, requirements and damage update live.

What's exact vs illustrative. The constellation tree, its costs and effects, the point budgets and the level-requirement curve are taken straight from the mod's code. The per-skill damage is an illustrative model on representative psycasts: real base values come from VPE plus whatever school add-ons you run, so treat damage as relative, not absolute.

Progression ties the build to the grind: schools used = path cards needed (one per enlightenment tier); cards deferred = card draws you would forgo (+4 specialization points each) to cover any spec-point shortfall; and the tier required and total cumulative meditation hours fold in the tiers for both your schools and those deferred cards. Casting and kills also supply spec points in play, so deferrals are an upper bound.
