Enlightenment & Transcendence

A psycaster's depth of enlightenment is a tier, carried as a hediff whose severity is the tier number. The first three tiers are a guided journey; beyond them lies an endless Transcendent climb with a geometric cost curve. Every tier makes future levels worth more.

The tier shows as a blue hediff on the Health tab and a small Roman-numeral badge beside the specialization-point star. It is read on any humanlike, so enemy psycasters can be Enlightened too.

Passive effects by tier

Everything a tier passively grants, at a glance. Flat psychic sensitivity comes from the hediff and caps at +35% (Illuminated on); the per-level rate and the breakthrough multiplier keep climbing with every tier.

Per-level rate multiplies the auto-stat growth (heat, recovery, sensitivity) for levels earned at that tier, non-retroactively. Breakthrough multiplies the meditation breakthrough chance, hard-capped at 60%.

Growth scaling: +15% per tier

Higher tiers make each new level you earn worth more. The per-level stat gains are multiplied by a rate that climbs 15% per tier.

Rate multiplier
RateMult(tier) = 1 + 0.15 · tier

Non-retroactive. A level is banked at the rate of the tier you were at when you earned it. Reaching a higher tier speeds up only your future levels - past levels keep their old value, so each point you gain in a new tier is permanently worth more than the ones before it.
The level cap rises with you. VPE caps a psycaster's level at 30. Each enlightenment tier raises that ceiling by +12 (so the cap is 30 + tier×12), and the balance target is that Tier 10 reaches level ~150 - enough to clear the level-15 requirement of even the deepest abilities, so a fully-maxed ascendancy is finishable by Tier 10. Keep climbing past it for more auto-stats, points and paths. At Tier 0 you are stuck at 30 until you Awaken - which is what makes the non-retroactive rate meaningful: new levels are earned at the higher tier's rate.

Transcendence: the endless climb

An Illuminated (Tier III) psycaster who keeps meditating accumulates progress toward the next Transcendent tier. The cost is geometric: each tier demands a fixed multiple of the previous one's meditation, so the journey slows dramatically - diminishing returns by design.

Meditation required for levels up to 150

Because the level cap rises with tier, unlocking higher psycaster levels is really a meditation cost. This is the cumulative meditation to open leveling all the way to each cap, from 30 to the level-150 ceiling - gentle through the guided tiers, then exponential through Transcendence.

What a Transcendent tier grants

  • +3 specialization points, bankable in the constellation tree.
  • An animated psycast-card pick - the same tarot-flip ceremony as the Awakening, drawing three cards from every path you have not yet claimed. Embrace one to unlock that whole path for free, or forgo the card for a further +4 specialization points (on top of the tier's +3).
  • The +15% rate keeps compounding, so deep Transcendents earn outsized levels - earned slowly, never runaway, because the climb above is geometric.
Three distinct systems, easy to tell apart: "Illuminated" is enlightenment Tier III (this page), "Convergence" is the apex specialization that opens the seven, and "Apotheosis" is the set of seven constellation identities.
