Psycaster Stats

Vanilla makes you spend points to upgrade neural heat, recovery and sensitivity. Psycasts² grows those three automatically every level - freeing your points for skill levels - and scales the growth with your enlightenment tier. Psyfocus cost is left untouched.

Base growth per level

StatPer level (base)Shown as
Neural heat limit+15+15/lvl
Neural heat recovery+0.0625/s+0.06/lvl
Psychic sensitivity+5%+5%/lvl
Psyfocus cost factorunchanged—

Each stat row in Modern Psycasts UI's left panel shows its live per-level gain as a right-aligned (+N/lvl), and the manual "Upgrade - Psycaster Stats" button is replaced by a note explaining the automation.

Tier scaling - non-retroactive

The growth rate climbs 15% per enlightenment tier. Each level is banked at the rate of the tier you held when you earned it - reaching a higher tier never rewrites past levels, it only speeds up future ones.

Accumulator (once, on each new level)
stat += base · RateMult(tiernow) RateMult(tier) = 1 + 0.15 · tier
basethe stat's tier-0 per-level amount (15 / 0.0625 / 0.05)
tiernowthe pawn's enlightenment tier when the level is gained

Worked example

A pawn reaches level 10 while unawakened (Tier 0), Awakens (Tier I), then earns levels 11-15. Neural-heat-limit offset:

Non-retroactive accumulation
levels 1-10 at ×1.00: 10 · 15 · 1.00 = 150 levels 11-15 at ×1.15: 5 · 15 · 1.15 = 86.25 ⇒ total offset = 236.25 # vs 258.75 if it were retroactive
The difference is the point. 236.25 vs 258.75 is exactly the early levels declining to be re-valued at the new tier - that is the non-retroactive rule, so points earned at higher tiers are permanently worth more.

Who it applies to

  • Tracked pawns (your awakened colonists) use the accumulator. The first time it runs it seeds existing levels at the Tier-0 rate, so loading a save never shifts your current stats.
  • Untracked psycasters (enemies, colonists before Awakening) use the flat Tier-0 rate - identical to a simple level × base, with no per-pawn bookkeeping stored.

The whole feature is one toggle (Auto psycaster stats); turning it off restores vanilla manual stat upgrades.
