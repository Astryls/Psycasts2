The Idea

Learning a psycast is just the first level.

Vanilla psycasters learn an ability and that is the end of its growth. Psycasts² reads whatever paths you have installed - vanilla, every add-on, third-party - and layers an RPG on top: levels, directed synergies, a constellation tree, seven lore ascensions, and infinite enlightenment. No per-mod patches.

⚛

Diablo-style levels

Every cast levels 1-10 (15 with Synthesis). Each level raises one primary stat by a fixed raw amount.

↔

Directed synergies

Path-mates empower one another. Leveling a skill's neighbours feeds its damage, area, cost, or cooldown.

✨

Endless enlightenment

Awakened to Ascended through pilgrimage, then an endless Transcendent climb that keeps paying off.
